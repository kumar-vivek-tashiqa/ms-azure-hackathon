  • Is KYC Mandatory?

    The requirement is mandatory. An investor who wishes to open a new account in a mutual fund where he/she has never invested before is required to complete the formalities in order to enable them to invest.

  • Who can avail the SIP facility?

    An existing individual investor can register SIP online. Investor need to have Net banking with utility payment facility enabled with bank registered in the portfolio.

  • How long does it take to complete the KYC formalities?

    It takes upto 3 business days to complete the KYC formalities if complete and proper documentation is received. However, Investor may start transacting immediately on receipt of the KYC acknowledgement letter.

  • What are the tax benefits?

    Equity Linked Savings Scheme (ELSS) is a type of mutual fund scheme that can provide tax benefits. It also has a lock in period of 3 years.

  • Can I switch between funds?

    You may switch all or part of your investments in one fund to another available fund. AMCs do not charge fees for such switches. To process a switch, you need to provide clear instructions, by completing a online form.

  • What are the inherent risks in mutual funds?

    Equity Funds are open to market risks; the price of the stocks in which the fund has invested may reduce. Conversely, the prices may go up, enabling the funds to earn profits. Debts Funds are open to credit and interest rate risks.
