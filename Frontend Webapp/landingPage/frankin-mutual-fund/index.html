Small investments for bigger dreams

SIP or Systematic Investment Plan is a plan through which a person can invest a small amount in a mutual fund at regular intervals (monthly/quarterly).SIP averages your investment cost over the investment duration and gives the flexibility of choosing your own amount and frequency, making it an ideal investment option for any investor.

Since your investment amount gets broken down in equal instalments, your investments average out the market ups and downs resulting in averaging your cost.
